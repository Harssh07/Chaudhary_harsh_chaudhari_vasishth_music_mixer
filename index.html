How to use

Step 1: Access the Music Mixer Open the website hosting the Music Mixer application. You will see the Music Mixer interface with icons and empty containers.

Step 2: Mix Audio Elements Drag an icon from the icons section. Drop the icon into one of the empty containers.

Step 3: Play the Audio Mix Click on a container with an icon inside. The audio for that track will start playing.

Step 4: Reset and Pause Click the "Reset" button to remove all icons from the containers. Click the "Pause" button to pause the audio playback, click again to resume.
